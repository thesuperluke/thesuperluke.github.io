Hobbies & Interests

Art

I enjoy expressing creativity through visual art, particularly painting and drawing. These activities allow me to explore colour, composition, and visual storytelling, while also providing a relaxing and rewarding contrast to technical work. I enjoy experimenting with different styles and techniques and continuously developing my artistic skills.

Music

Music is another important creative outlet for me. I enjoy playing guitar and learning new pieces across a range of genres. Playing music helps develop discipline, patience, and attention to detail, while also providing an enjoyable way to relax and unwind.

Sport

I am passionate about staying active through sport, particularly tennis and golf. Tennis appeals to me because of its combination of physical fitness, strategy, and quick decision-making. Golf provides a different challenge, requiring focus, precision, and consistency over longer periods of play. Both sports help me maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
